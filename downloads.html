Windows 98 Dream Edition is a remaster of a well known OS - Windows 98

It's purpose is for installing it on more modern hardware. Dream Edition provides custom patches including:

USB Supplement, KernelEx (may be buggy at the moment), PatchMEM. Some starter programs: XMPlay, Paint Shop Pro, and even a game -- "Holdover"

You can customize your desktop like in your dreams with multiple featured wallpapers by: blurry, Razorback members and some awesome uncredited photographers.
